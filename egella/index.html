egella group branding



EGELLA Group is Dubai, UAE based, specialized in sourcing and sale of a diversified portfolio of products and represents an array of worldwide manufacturers of agricultural, dairy and other food products such as fresh fruits, vegetables, dried fruits, cheese, olives and oils, honey as well as non-food products like home cleaners, personal care and leather care.



We've been asked to create a brand identity and a website for their business. They have the expertise to source a wide range of products to and from anywhere. To symbolize this, we designed the logo in a circular motion completed with an e shape.
